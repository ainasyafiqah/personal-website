    Book Review

    IT ENDS WITH US

    When I first encounter this book, it was actually recommended by a lot of book reviewers on TikTok. At first, the reviews that I have watched does not convince me enough. However, after seeing the reviews on goodread, I have decided to read them. I love physical book better than e-book version because I feel like it is much better having the book on my hand. I also wanted to increase my novels collections, hence I bought the book on book depository website. It took about 2 weeks to arrive since it is pre-order book. The waiting was crucial for me since I was really impatient and I just can't wait to read the book. After it has arrived, I straight away read them. It took me about 4 days to finish the book since the pages are average and not a lot for me. For me, I really like this book because it talks about abusive relationship whereby I think some of us can relate to. It is not unsual things in life but definitely not a good thing either. I just love how Lily face all of her marriage problems since she got an abusive husband. She used to lived in abusive household because of her father. She have witnessed all of the abuse that her mom have gotten from her dad ever since she was little. So, reading this book really open my eyes about abusive relationship and not only romantic stuff. Life is definitely full of unexpected things just like how LIly never expected for her husband to be abusive after their marriage. This novel also talk about what you can do if you have ever encounter an abusive relationship. As a woman myself, I think the Lily character is really inspiring. Hence, in my opinion the writer did a great job by talking about this issues even in her novels.
